# Karunada Vanya (National Pride) 🍃🐅

**Karunada Vanya** is a Wildlife Education & Alert application developed for the people who live where the forest begins. It aims to foster "Wildlife Pride" and educate children on the ecological value of Karnataka's flora and fauna while providing safety tools for residents in forest-fringe villages.

## 🌟 Vision
- **Wonder:** Educating the next generation about Karnataka's rich biodiversity.
- **Safety:** Providing real-time community-driven alerts to reduce human-wildlife conflict.

## 🚀 Features

### 📖 Wildlife Wiki
An offline-ready encyclopedia of Karnataka's iconic species:
- Black Panther (The ghost of Kabini)
- Asian Elephant
- Bengal Tiger
- Great Indian Hornbill
- Sandalwood Tree
- Malabar Giant Squirrel

### 🚨 Movement Alerts
A "Community Ping" system allowing residents to report sightings (e.g., Leopard movements or Elephant raids) to alert neighbors. 
- **Auto-expiry:** Alerts automatically expire after 6 hours to ensure data relevance.

### 🛡️ Co-existence Guide
Practical safety tips and protocols for when wildlife enters human settlements.
- Safety measures for Elephant encounters.
- Protection steps for Leopard sightings near villages.

### 🔊 Forest Sounds
Simulated high-quality audio clips of birds and animals to bring the experience of the wild to the user's fingertips.

## 🛠️ Technical Details
- **Platform:** Android
- **Language:** Kotlin
- **UI Architecture:** Material 3 with a custom Nature-inspired theme.
- **Navigation:** Modern Activity-based navigation with smooth transitions.

## 🎨 UI Design
The app features a creative, artistic interface inspired by the natural beauty of Karnataka:
- **Serif Typography** for a premium educational feel.
- **Nature-inspired Color Palette:** Forest Green, Vanya Gold, and Earth Brown.
- **Decorative Elements:** Stylized floating icons and artistic headers.
